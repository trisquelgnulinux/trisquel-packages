Bits of freedom

Our project is committed to provide you with nothing but libre software, following the Free System Distribution Guidelines. These guidelines extend the free software definition to ensure you can use, study, modify and share every piece of Trisquel.

You can also support free software by becoming a Free Software Foundation member, and if you do they will send you a Trisquel Live USB card and a DVD full of our source code.

We promise Trisquel will always be free as in freedom, but if we make a mistake and you find something inappropriate, tell us and we will fix it.
